Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026

This policy explains how [Your registered business name] ("BillFlow", "we") handles information in connection with the BillFlow application and website (getbillflow.github.io).

The short version: BillFlow runs on your own PC. Your business data — your bills, customers, and payments — stays on your computer. We do not collect it. We only hold the details you give us when you buy or ask for support.

1. Your business & customer data stays with you

BillFlow is on-premise software. Your Tally data, customer list, bills, statements and WhatsApp session are stored locally on your computer. This information is not uploaded to us and we have no access to it.

2. Information we collect

We only collect the limited information you provide directly when you purchase or contact us, such as your name, business name, email address, and phone number. When you buy online, your payment is processed by Razorpay and we receive a confirmation of payment.

3. How we use it

  • To issue and manage your licence/activation key.
  • To send your invoice and payment receipt.
  • To provide support, updates, and renewal reminders.

4. Payments

Online payments are handled securely by Razorpay Software Pvt. Ltd. We do not receive or store your full card, UPI, or bank credentials. Razorpay's handling of your payment data is governed by Razorpay's own privacy policy.

5. Third-party services

BillFlow works with services that you control and configure — Tally Prime, your own WhatsApp number, and optionally an email provider. Those services process data under their own privacy terms. We do not receive that data.

6. Sharing

We do not sell or rent your information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (for example, our payment processor), or where required by law.

7. Data retention

We keep the purchase and support information you provide for as long as needed to serve you and to meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your details.

9. Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, subject to legal requirements. Contact us using the details below.

10. Website & cookies

Our website is a simple informational site hosted on GitHub Pages. It does not run advertising trackers. Basic technical logs may be collected by the hosting provider.

11. Changes

We may update this policy; the latest version is always published on this page.
