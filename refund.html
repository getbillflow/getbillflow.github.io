Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026
Try before you buy. BillFlow gives you a full 7-day free trial — the complete product with no watermarks — so you can confirm it works for your shop before paying anything. We strongly encourage you to use the trial first.

1. Subscriptions

BillFlow is sold as an annual subscription to digital software, delivered as a download plus an activation key. Your subscription is active from the date the key is issued for a period of one year.

2. Refunds

Because BillFlow is digital software delivered with a licence key, and because a free trial is available to evaluate it beforehand, subscription fees are generally non-refundable once the activation key has been issued.

We will consider a refund in genuine cases such as:

  • A duplicate or accidental payment.
  • Payment made but no activation key delivered.
  • A verified technical issue that prevents the software from running on a supported setup and which we are unable to resolve.

Refund requests must be raised within 7 days of payment. Approved refunds are made to the original payment method, typically within 5–7 working days.

3. Cancellation / non-renewal

You can cancel simply by choosing not to renew. When your annual subscription ends, the application pauses until renewed; your data stays safely on your computer. There is no auto-charge — each year's renewal is a fresh payment you choose to make.

4. Invoice

An invoice is issued by email for every payment.

5. How to request a refund or cancellation

Email getbillflow@gmail.com or call/WhatsApp +91 76981 13888 with your business name and payment reference. We will respond within 2 working days.
